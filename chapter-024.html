🎯 WHAT COULD BE DONE (Strategy for Future-Prediction Texts)

The Temporal Logic Protocol

Step 1: SCAN for all time markers
  → "demain," "aujourd'hui," "déjà," "bientôt," "dans X ans"
  → Underline them. They're your time anchors.

Step 2: LABEL each section's time zone
  → Past / Present / Near Future / Far Future

Step 3: For each gap, determine its TIME ZONE
  → What time is BEFORE? What time is AFTER?
  → The gap must create a SMOOTH transition between them

Step 4: ELIMINATE answers in the wrong time zone
  → A past-tense answer in a future-looking gap = WRONG
  → A "never" answer before "already exists" = CONTRADICTION

Step 5: VERIFY the temporal zoom direction
  → Zoom IN (future → present): confirms vision is real
  → Zoom OUT (present → future): shows how much further we'll go
